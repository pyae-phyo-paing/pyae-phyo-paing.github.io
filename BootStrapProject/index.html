JMsolution

JMSolution is a South Korean beauty and skincare brand founded in 2012.
The company offers a wide range of products, including skincare, haircare, and makeup,with a focus on using innovative and premium ingredients. The brand aims to provide luxury skincare solutions that are both effective and accessible.

JMSolution's products are known for their fusion of Asian and Western
skincare traditions, and they emphasize sustainability in their production processes. Their products are vegan, cruelty-free, ocean-safe, and free from harmful chemicals like microplastics. This aligns with their commitment to both environmental responsibility and skin health.

The brand has gained popularity in both Asian and European markets, appealing to consumers who seek high-quality, sustainable beauty products​( JMsolution 2023,Tracxn).

Garnier

Garnier is a globally recognized beauty brand that specializes in skincare
and haircare products. Founded in France in 1904, Garnier has established
itself as a pioneer in the beauty industry, offering a wide range of products
that blend the best of nature and science. The brand is known for its commitment
to sustainability, using natural ingredients in its formulations and eco-friendly packaging.

Garnier's skincare line includes cleansers, moisturizers, serums, masks,
and sun protection products. Popular ranges include the Garnier Micellar Water,
known for gentle makeup removal, and the Garnier SkinActive line, which targets
various skin concerns using natural ingredients like green tea, rose water, and aloe vera.

Garnier has made significant strides in sustainability. The brand focuses on reducing its environmental impact by sourcing ingredients sustainably, using recyclable packaging, and reducing water usage in its products. Garnier is also committed to cruelty-free practices, and many of its products are vegan.

Vaseline

Vaseline is a well-known skincare brand with a rich history dating back to 1870.
The brand was founded by Robert Chesebrough, who discovered the benefits of petroleum jelly, a key ingredient in Vaseline products.

The name "Vaseline" has become synonymous with healing and moisturizing skin,
and it has been a staple in households for over a century. The original and most iconic product of Vaseline is its petroleum jelly. This versatile product is used for a variety of purposes, including healing dry skin, protecting minor cuts, and even as a lip balm. It is known for its ability to lock in moisture and create a protective barrier on the skin.

Vaseline offers a range of lotions and creams that are designed to provide deep moisture and care for dry skin. These products often contain ingredients like cocoa butter, aloe vera, and essential vitamins to nourish and hydrate the skin.

Vaseline is dedicated to promoting healthy skin. The brand is particularly focused on providing affordable skincare solutions that can be used by everyone, regardless of their skin type or needs. Vaseline's products are widely trusted for their efficacy in soothing, healing, and protecting the skin.

Nivea

Nivea is one of the world's leading skincare brands, with a history that spans over a century. Founded in Germany in 1911 by the company Beiersdorf, Nivea is known for pioneering skincare innovations and its commitment to providing high-quality products that cater to various skin types and needs.

The original Nivea Creme is one of the brand's most iconic products, known for its rich, thick formula that provides intensive moisture and care for the skin. Over the years, Nivea has expanded its range to include lighter lotions, creams, and body milks that are suitable for daily use.

Nivea offers a wide range of face care products, including cleansers, toners, moisturizers, and anti-aging creams. These products are designed to address different skin concerns, such as dryness, sensitivity, acne, and aging.

Nivea is also a leader in sun protection, offering a variety of sunscreens and after-sun care products. These include lotions, sprays, and creams with various SPF levels, designed to protect the skin from harmful UV rays. Nivea's dedication to quality and care has made it a trusted brand in households around the world, known for delivering reliable skincare solutions that help people feel confident and comfortable in their skin.

Dr.G

Dr.G is a South Korean skincare brand known for its dermatologically tested products that cater to various skin concerns. Established by Dr. Ahn Gun-Young, a dermatologist with years of experience, the brand's philosophy centers around providing safe, effective, and gentle skincare solutions.

Dr.G’s Cica line is formulated with Centella Asiatica, an ingredient known for its soothing and healing properties. These products are designed for sensitive and irritated skin, helping to calm redness and strengthen the skin barrier.

This range focuses on intense hydration and moisture retention. Products in this line include hyaluronic acid-rich formulations that deeply moisturize the skin, making it plump and smooth.

Dr.G is well-known for its sun protection products, particularly the Green Mild Up Sun SPF50+. These sunscreens are formulated to provide broad-spectrum protection against UV rays while being gentle on the skin, making them suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
