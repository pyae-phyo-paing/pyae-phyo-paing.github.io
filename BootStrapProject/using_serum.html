How To Use Serum?

Using Serum

Using a serum in your skincare routine is simple, and it can offer deep hydration, anti-aging benefits, and other targeted skin improvements. Here’s how to use a serum effectively:
- Start with clean, dry skin. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser to remove dirt, oil, and makeup.
- Take a small amount of serum (2-3 drops) and gently apply it to your face and neck. You can use your fingers or palms, pressing it into the skin instead of rubbing.
- Focus on areas where you want more attention, such as wrinkles, dark spots, or dry patches.
- Give the serum a minute or two to fully absorb into your skin before moving on to the next step.
- Lock in the serum’s active ingredients with a moisturizer to ensure your skin stays hydrated and protected.
- Depending on the type of serum, use it once or twice daily (morning and night).
Serums are lightweight and packed with active ingredients, making them a key step in targeting specific skin concerns like aging, brightening, or hydration.
