How To Use Moisturizer?

Using Moisturizer

Using a moisturizer is essential for keeping your skin hydrated and maintaining its barrier function. Here's how you can properly use a moisturizer:
- Wash your face using a gentle cleanser suited to your skin type (oily, dry, sensitive, etc.). Pat dry your skin with a clean towel, leaving it slightly damp.
- Use the Right Amount: Generally, a pea-sized amount is enough for your entire face. For the body, you may need more depending on the area you're moisturizing.
- Dot the Moisturizer: Apply small dots of moisturizer across your face (forehead, cheeks, chin, nose).
- Massage Gently: Use upward, circular motions to spread the moisturizer evenly over your face. Avoid tugging or pulling your skin.
- Pay extra attention to dry areas or spots that need more hydration (like around the nose or mouth).
- Let the moisturizer fully absorb into your skin before applying any other skincare products or makeup.
- Moisturize in the morning and evening as part of your daily skincare routine.
- Use a Product for Your Skin Type: Choose oil-free or gel-based moisturizers for oily skin, and richer creams for dry skin.
