How To Use Cleansing Water?

Using Cleansing Water

Cleansing water, often known as micellar water, is a gentle yet effective way to cleanse the skin. Here's how to use it:
- Start with dry skin. No need to wet your face beforehand.
- Pour a generous amount of cleansing water onto a cotton pad until itâ€™s damp but not soaking.
- Gently swipe the cotton pad across your face, focusing on areas with makeup, dirt, or oil. For stubborn makeup, press the pad on the area for a few seconds before wiping.
- If needed, use a second pad to ensure your skin is completely clean.
- Most cleansing waters do not require rinsing, making it convenient for quick cleansing.
- After cleansing, apply your moisturizer or other skincare products as needed.
This method effectively removes makeup and impurities while keeping your skin hydrated.
